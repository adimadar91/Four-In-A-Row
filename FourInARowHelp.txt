Four in a row instructions:

This game is played by two players,
The objective of the game is to connect four of one's own discs of the same 
color next to each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent.
Connect Four is a strongly solved game.
The first player can always win by playing the right moves.

At this app, you first of all insert your names and the size of board
that you want.
then, every turn the current player have to choose a col and insert his disk.
you can start an new tournir/game and set again your properties using the "properties" 
option in the menu bar.

Enjoy!
